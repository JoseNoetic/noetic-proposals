01

Brief & Competitive Landscape

We build the strategic foundation: business challenge, category insight, competitive teardown across paid, organic, and messaging. By Day 14, you have a written brief that names the white space — and we both agree on what we're solving for.

Audience segments mapped to motivations, channels, and stages. For B2B: buying committees with role-specific narratives. For DTC: named buyer personas tied to creative briefs. For B2C2B: dual-audience frameworks — consumer and trade.

Launch & Continuous Optimization

Daily monitoring, weekly tactical optimizations, monthly strategic reviews. Quarterly business reviews tied to commercial outcomes — not impressions, not reach, not vanity metrics.
